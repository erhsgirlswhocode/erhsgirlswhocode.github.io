What are we?

The Girls Who Code club at Eleanor Roosevelt High School (eSTEM) is dedicated to inspiring the next generation of innovators through creativity, collaboration, and code! Through workshops, events, and peer learning, we encourage students to see themselves as innovators and leaders in STEM.

Workshops

Beginner-friendly lessons, projects, and hands-on coding practice.

Hour of Code

We organize and teach Hour of Code sessions for students and schools.

Community

A team that supports each other—learning, leadership, and outreach.
