Hour of Code

Over the past few years, our student volunteers have introduced computer science to more than 1,000 students across 30+ classrooms in our local community. This year, we’re excited to continue our Hour of Code outreach program, where high school coders visit elementary and middle schools to lead 60-minute interactive coding sessions. Each session features hands-on activities designed to build logical thinking, creativity, and teamwork. No prior coding experience is required. All participants will receive an Hour of Code certificate to celebrate their learning and participation.

FAQ

How long is a session?

Usually ~45–60 minutes, depending on the class schedule.

Do students need experience?

No. We design sessions for beginners.

What do teachers need to prepare?

A classroom + devices (if possible). We will email you the lesson slideshow before the session.
